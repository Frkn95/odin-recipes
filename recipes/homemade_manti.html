Homemade Manti (Turkish Dumplings)

Description

Manti is a type of dumpling.

The dumplings typically consist of a spiced meat mixture, wrapped in a thin dough sheet which is then boiled

Ingredients

Dough:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon water

Filling:

  • 2 onions
  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • salt and pepper

Red Pepper Oil:

  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon red pepper flakes

Yogurt Sauce:

  • 8 ounce plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
